Emilio China aka Zef Noi$e (Zef Noise)

Emilio China, known variously as E. Zef, E.China, E.Zef, Emilio, Emilio (Zef) China, Emilio DiZefalo and Emilio Zef China, is a singer, violin player, composer and multi-instrumentalist who co-founded the pioneering avant-funk, third stream and hip-hop/industrial fusion act Sweet Lizard Illtet with Mike Kilmer (aka Mike iLL) and released a self-titled album with Warner Brothers Records in 1991.

He has at various times recorded and/or performed with Jonathan Davis (Korn), Firewater, Peter Murphy, Genesis P-Orridge, Psychic TV and countless other musical and theatrical artists.
